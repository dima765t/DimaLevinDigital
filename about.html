About the studio

A small studio obsessed with the quiet details.

DimaLevin Digital exists for one reason — to give small businesses websites that look premium, perform reliably and convert visitors into customers.

Manifesto

We believe a website is a hard-working employee, not a billboard. It should qualify leads, tell the story, reassure the skeptic and get out of the way.

We believe speed is design. Typography is design. Trust is design. Every technical decision we make is a design decision.

We believe a studio should be small enough to care and senior enough to deliver. That's why you will always work directly with the people building your site.

The approach

Three principles that guide every decision, from kickoff to launch.

01

Clarity over cleverness

A confused visitor is a lost customer. We lead with what a business does, for whom, and why it matters — then let the craft carry the tone.

02

Speed as a feature

Every site ships with Core Web Vitals in the green. Hand-written code, aggressive image handling, no unnecessary JavaScript.

03

Long after launch

Launch is day one. We monitor, iterate, and tune the site monthly — because websites should age well, not go stale.

Process

Six phases. One conversation from start to finish.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    A short, focused workshop to map business goals, customer archetypes and competitive landscape.

  2. 02

    Architecture

    Sitemap, user flows and wireframes. We commit to structure before we commit to style.

  3. 03

    Visual system

    Typography, color, spacing, motion — a system that scales across every page you'll ever add.

  4. 04

    Build

    Hand-coded, accessible, fast. Reviewed on real devices, not just the designer's laptop.

  5. 05

    Launch

    QA, analytics wiring, SEO foundations and a calm deployment. No surprise Monday morning.

  6. 06

    Evolve

    Ongoing care, iteration and new sections as your business changes. Month after month.
