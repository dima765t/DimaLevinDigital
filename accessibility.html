Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

DimaLevin Digital is committed to making this website accessible and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.

Legal basis

Israeli accessibility law

This website operates in accordance with the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Law (5758-1998) and the Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Regulations (Service Accessibility Adjustments) (5773-2013). We strive to conform to Level AA of Israeli Standard IS 5568, which is based on WCAG 2.0 Level AA.

Status

Conformance level

Partial conformance

This website partially conforms to WCAG 2.0 Level AA. Some content or functionality may not yet fully meet all criteria.

What we've done

Accessibility measures

The following accessibility features are implemented across this site:

  • Semantic HTML5 structure with logical heading hierarchy
  • ARIA landmark roles, labels and descriptions throughout
  • Full keyboard navigation support
  • Language and reading-direction attributes for Hebrew (RTL) and English (LTR)
  • Descriptive alternative text on all meaningful images
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios
  • Responsive layout for all screen sizes and zoom levels
  • Reduced motion support (respects prefers-reduced-motion)
  • Visible focus indicators for keyboard users
  • Skip-to-main-content link on every page

Limitations

Known limitations

Third-party services integrated into this site — including external fonts (Google Fonts) and linked platforms (WhatsApp) — may not fully meet all accessibility requirements. These are outside our direct control. We continue to review and improve the site's accessibility on an ongoing basis.

Contact

Accessibility feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on this website, or if you need content in an alternative format, please contact us — we will do our best to respond promptly and address the issue.
